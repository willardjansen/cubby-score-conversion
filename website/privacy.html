Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

CubbyScore is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle data when you use our website and software.

Data Collection

Website: Our website is a simple static site. We do not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies. No data is collected when you visit our website.

Software: The CubbyScore desktop application runs entirely on your local computer. It:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not send data to external servers
  • Does not track your usage
  • Processes all PDF files locally on your machine
  • Never uploads your music scores anywhere

Your PDF files and converted MusicXML files never leave your computer. All processing happens locally using the bundled Audiveris OMR engine.

Cookies

Our website does not use cookies. No consent banner is required because we simply don't track visitors.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • GitHub - Source code hosting and download distribution

The CubbyScore application does not connect to any external services during operation. All music recognition is performed locally using open-source software (Audiveris, music21).

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. Since we don't collect data, there is nothing to share.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your music files remain exclusively on your computer under your full control.

Open Source

CubbyScore is open source software. You can review the complete source code on GitHub to verify that no data collection occurs in the application.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on GitHub.
